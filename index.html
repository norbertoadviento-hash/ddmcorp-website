We Build From Drawings — Yours Or Ours

Every fabrication job starts with a spec, whether that's a drawing you hand us or one we draft in-house before steel gets cut. Dimensions, tolerances, and anchor points are locked before fabrication starts, so what goes up matches what was engineered.

  • Custom structural drawings for canopies, rails, and facility structures
  • CNC-cut steel fabricated to the drawing, not eyeballed on site
  • Anchor and load specs confirmed before install begins
